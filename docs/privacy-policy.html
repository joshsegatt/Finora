Privacy Policy for Finora

Last updated: November 12, 2024

Finora ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Finora mobile application (the "Service").

TL;DR: Your financial data stays on YOUR device. We don't sell, share, or store your expense data on external servers. Your privacy is our priority.

Information Collection and Use

Personal Data We Collect

  • Expense Data: Amount, description, merchant, category, date, notes
  • Receipt Images: Photos taken or selected for OCR processing (processed locally on your device, NOT uploaded to servers)
  • Usage Preferences: Currency, language, dark mode, notification settings
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version (anonymous, for crash reports only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain expense tracking functionality
  • To categorize transactions using on-device AI (Google ML Kit)
  • To generate financial insights and budget reports
  • To send notifications about budgets and spending alerts
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage

  • ✅ All expense data is stored locally on your device
  • ✅ No financial data is transmitted to external servers
  • ✅ Cloud backup (if you enable) uses your Google account encryption
  • ✅ We NEVER have access to your backup data

Third-Party Services

We integrate the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., "how many users opened the app today")
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports to fix bugs (no personal or financial data included)
  • Google ML Kit: On-device OCR for receipt scanning (images never leave your device)
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases (handled entirely by Google, we don't see payment info)

Data Retention

  • Data is retained until you delete the app or manually clear app data
  • Exported CSV files remain on your device until you manually delete them
  • Analytics data is retained by Firebase for 14 months (anonymous)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your expenses as CSV (Settings → Export to CSV)
  • Delete: Uninstall the app or go to Settings → Apps → Finora → Clear Data
  • Opt-out Analytics: Settings → Notifications → Disable analytics (coming soon)

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Local database encryption (SQLCipher)
  • Secure network connections (HTTPS only)
  • No third-party data sharing or selling
  • Regular security audits

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International Users

Finora is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally, no data crosses borders unless you manually export/share it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here and notified in the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU:

  • Right to Access: Export your data anytime (Settings → Export CSV)
  • Right to Erasure: Uninstall or clear app data
  • Right to Portability: CSV exports work with any spreadsheet software
  • Legal Basis: Processing is based on consent and legitimate interest (app functionality)

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • We do NOT sell your personal information
  • You can request data deletion (clear app data)
  • You can opt-out of analytics (Settings)
